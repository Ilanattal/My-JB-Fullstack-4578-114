Why Ilan's Food ?

In 2022, 43% of adults aged 18 and over were overweight and 16% were obese.
More than 390 million children and adolescents aged 5 to 19 were overweight in 2022.
In order to combat these statistics, we have created tailor-made menus with less than 700 calories per meal for easy and pleasant weight loss.

Our advices to your weight loss :
  1. Eat a balanced diet: Prioritize nutrient-rich foods, such as vegetables, fruits, lean proteins (fish, chicken, tofu), and good fats (avocados, nuts, olive oil). Limit processed foods and foods high in added sugars.
  2. Hydrate: Drinking water before meals can help reduce appetite and prevent dehydration, often confused with hunger. Try to drink around 1.5 to 2 liters of water per day.
  3. Exercise regularly: Combine cardio training (like walking, running, or swimming) with muscle-strengthening exercises (like weight training or yoga) to increase your metabolism and burn more calories.
  4. Be patient and persistent: Weight loss is a process that takes time. Don't get discouraged if you don't see immediate results. Stay motivated and focus on your overall well-being.
